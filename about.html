OUR STORY

From a small group chat to a global community

Code Investors began in 2019 as a handful of self-taught developers trading notes and code reviews after work. There was no course, no curriculum — just people helping each other get unstuck and ship better software.

That habit of showing up for each other never went away. As the group grew, it turned into structured projects, mentorship circles and events, but the core idea stayed the same: developers grow faster together than alone.

What makes us different is simple — we optimize for building, not talking. Every member is encouraged to ship something, ask for feedback, and pass on what they've learned to the next person joining the community.

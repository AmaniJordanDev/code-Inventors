ABOUT US

Where Developers Become Innovators

Code Investors started as a small group of self-taught developers sharing notes after work. Today, it's a global community where people learn in public, ship real projects and grow their careers together.

Whether you're writing your first line of code or leading engineering teams, there's a place for you here — no gatekeeping, just people who genuinely enjoy building things.

  • ✓ Open-source collaboration on real products
  • ✓ Mentorship from senior engineers
  • ✓ A community that celebrates shipping, not just talking
